Test Your Hand Strength

Hello! Welcome to the Hand Strength Test! This site will show you how to rate your opening hand in Texas Hold 'Em based on the Chen Formula. to calculate your opening hand strength using the Chen Formula, first: score only your highest card. A=10, K=8, Q=7, J=6, 10 through 2= half of card value. Multiply pairs by 2 of one card's value. Minimum score for a pair is 5. Add 2 points if the cards are suited. Subtract points for gaps between cards. No gap= 0, 1 card gap= -1, 2 card gap= -2, 3 card gap= -4, 4 or more= -5. Aces are high for gap calculations so there would be a 4+ card gap for a hand with an ace and a three. Add 1 point if there is a 0-1 gap and both cards are lower than a Queen. Then round half point scores up. For more information go to: https://www.thepokerbank.com/strategy/basic/starting-hand-selection/chen-formula/
